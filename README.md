# 📧 Phishing Detection & Awareness Report

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing real-world phishing email samples and identifying key indicators used by attackers to deceive users. The goal is to simulate a real Security Operations Center (SOC) workflow by classifying email risks and creating awareness guidelines to help prevent phishing attacks.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze phishing email samples
- Identify common phishing indicators
- Classify email risk levels (Safe / Suspicious / Phishing)
- Explain attack techniques in simple language
- Create user awareness and prevention guidelines

---

## 📂 Project Contents
- 📄 Phishing Analysis Report (PDF)
- 🖼️ Sample Email Screenshots
- 📝 Detailed analysis of 3 email samples

---

## 🔍 Analysis Approach
The following steps were followed during analysis:

1. Collected phishing email samples  
2. Reviewed email subject, sender, and content  
3. Identified phishing indicators (red flags)  
4. Analyzed links and attachments  
5. Classified risk levels  
6. Documented findings in a structured report  
7. Created awareness and prevention guidelines  

---

## 🚨 Key Phishing Indicators Identified
- Suspicious or mismatched sender domains  
- Use of urgency and fear tactics  
- Presence of malicious links or attachments  
- Generic greetings and lack of personalization  
- Financial or security-related bait  
- Call-to-action buttons leading to unverified pages  

---

## ⚠️ Risk Classification
- 🔴 **Phishing (High Risk)** – Clear malicious intent  
- 🟡 **Suspicious (Medium Risk)** – Requires further verification  
- 🟢 **Safe (Low Risk)** – No major threats detected  

---

## 🛡️ Prevention Guidelines
- Verify sender email addresses carefully  
- Avoid clicking on unknown links or attachments  
- Access accounts only through official websites  
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)  
- Report suspicious emails immediately  

---

## 🧠 Key Learnings
- Phishing attacks rely heavily on social engineering  
- Human awareness is the first line of defense  
- Even well-designed emails can be malicious  
- Proper analysis helps reduce false positives and improve detection
